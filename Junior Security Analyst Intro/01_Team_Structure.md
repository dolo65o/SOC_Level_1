## SOC Team Structure & Junior Analyst Role

### SOC Team Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| **Junior SOC Analyst** | First responder — triage alerts, handle tickets, detect/prevent threats |
| **Senior Analyst** | Handle complex attacks, mentor juniors, deeper investigations |
| **SOC Engineer** | Configure and maintain security tools (SIEM, EDR, IDS, etc.) |
| **SOC Manager** | Oversee operations, coordinate team, report to CISO |

---

### Junior Analyst — Daily Duties

Real-world tasks you'll face:
- Detect and prevent data stealer infections on endpoints
- Analyze and stop phishing campaigns
- Participate in large-scale incidents (ransomware response)
- Build detection rules and automations with the team
- Understand business operations — not just the tech

---

### Reality of the Job

```
8-hour shift =
  → Mountain of tickets to triage
  → Constant context switching
  → Must stay current on new TTPs
  → Occasionally stop a real threat = worth it
```

**Hard truth:**
> Most of your alerts will be false positives.
> Your job is to find the real ones buried inside the noise.
> Speed matters — but accuracy matters more.
> One missed true positive can mean a full-scale breach.

---

### Why It's Worth It
- You see how real attacks from the news actually work, up close
- Every incident teaches something new
- Defensive work builds a foundation for any cybersecurity career path
- Direct, measurable impact — you either stopped it or you didn't
